Privacy Policy

 

1.General

We value your privacy and protecting your privacy is critical to us. We are happy to tell you about the type and usage of data collected by our company and to help you understand how Viddup handles the personally identifiable information ("PII") that you occasionally provide over the Internet.

 

2. Collection, use and processing of personal information

When you download Viddup from the apple appstore, we do not collect any personally identifiable information about you. This includes, but is not limited to, your location information. The calculations generated during the use of the product are limited to local use and will not be uploaded to any online server. At the same time, we will not track the video or image information generated by users when using our products, and this information will only be saved in your ios system album.

 

We only obtain personally identifiable information when you decide to send us your feedback or submit an error report. The content obtained is limited to the image, video and text of your name, email and reply. We guarantee that your PII will only be used to contact you and improve our services. We will never disclose your personally identifiable information to third parties for commercial gain.

We use Facebook as an advertiser. We will be advertising Viddup on Facebook and Instagram. For more information, please visit https://developers.facebook.com/docs/privacy

 

If you are concerned about your PII being abused, or if you would like more information about our privacy policy and its implications, please feel free to email us at  viddup_support@xiaoxiekeji.cn  and we will answer your questions.
